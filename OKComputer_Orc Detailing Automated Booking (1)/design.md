# Orc Detailing - Design Style Guide

## Design Philosophy

### Visual Language
- **Automotive Excellence**: Clean, precision-focused design that reflects the meticulous nature of professional car detailing
- **Premium Quality**: Sophisticated aesthetic that conveys luxury service and attention to detail
- **Modern Professionalism**: Contemporary design that builds trust and credibility
- **Performance-Driven**: Visual elements that suggest speed, efficiency, and exceptional results

### Color Palette
- **Primary**: Deep Charcoal (#2C2C2C) - Professional, automotive industry standard
- **Secondary**: Platinum Silver (#E5E5E5) - Clean, metallic finish association
- **Accent**: Ocean Blue (#1E3A8A) - Trust, reliability, depth
- **Highlight**: Copper Orange (#EA580C) - Energy, premium service, warmth
- **Background**: Pure White (#FFFFFF) - Cleanliness, precision

### Typography
- **Display Font**: "Playfair Display" - Elegant serif for headings, premium feel
- **Body Font**: "Inter" - Clean, modern sans-serif for readability
- **Accent Font**: "JetBrains Mono" - Technical precision for pricing and specifications

## Visual Effects & Styling

### Hero Section
- **Background**: Cinematic car detailing imagery with subtle overlay
- **Text Treatment**: Large, bold headings with gradient effects
- **Animation**: Typewriter effect for main headline
- **Layout**: Split-screen with image and text content

### Interactive Elements
- **Service Cards**: Hover effects with 3D tilt and shadow expansion
- **Booking Calendar**: Smooth transitions with date highlighting
- **Form Elements**: Floating labels with focus animations
- **Buttons**: Gradient backgrounds with hover state transformations

### Animation Library Usage
- **Anime.js**: Smooth transitions, stagger animations for service cards
- **Typed.js**: Dynamic text effects for hero section
- **Splitting.js**: Letter-by-letter animations for headings
- **ECharts.js**: Service comparison visualizations
- **Splide.js**: Image carousels for before/after galleries

### Background Effects
- **Shader-park**: Subtle particle system suggesting water droplets or polish
- **PIXI.js**: Liquid metal displacement effects for premium feel
- **p5.js**: Geometric patterns inspired by automotive design

### Layout & Flow
- **Grid System**: CSS Grid with consistent spacing and alignment
- **Scroll Behavior**: Smooth scrolling with reveal animations
- **Mobile-First**: Responsive design optimized for all devices
- **Visual Hierarchy**: Clear information architecture with proper contrast ratios

### Component Styling
- **Navigation**: Fixed header with glass morphism effect
- **Service Cards**: Elevated design with pricing badges
- **Booking Interface**: Clean, form-focused layout with progress indicators
- **Footer**: Minimal design with essential contact information

### Image Treatment
- **Hero Images**: High-quality automotive photography with no color overlays
- **Service Images**: Consistent aspect ratios with subtle border radius
- **Gallery Images**: Before/after comparisons with hover reveal effects
- **Background Images**: Subtle textures and patterns supporting content

### Accessibility Considerations
- **Color Contrast**: Minimum 4.5:1 ratio for all text
- **Focus States**: Clear visual indicators for keyboard navigation
- **Alt Text**: Descriptive text for all images
- **Screen Reader**: Semantic HTML structure with proper ARIA labels
